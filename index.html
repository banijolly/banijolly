About Me

I am a dedicated genomics researcher specializing in the dynamic field of genomic epidemiology. My work is driven by a collective mission to harness the power of cutting-edge Genomics and Data Analytics for making a meaningful impact on the world around us.

Projects

genepi-box

Genetic Epidemiology in a Box (genepi-box), an open-source toolkit developed to automate the assembly and analysis of SARS-CoV-2 genomes for genetic epidemiology and surveillance

Genetic Epidemiology of Novel and Emerging Pathogens in India (Genepi)

Resources for the use of high-throughput genomics approaches to understand genomic epidemiology SARS-CoV-2 and other novel and emerging pathogens in India
WHO-SEARO SARS-CoV-2 Sequencing and Analysis

World Health Organization-led initiative to enhance genomic sequencing and bioinformatic analysis of SARS-CoV-2 in South-East Asia Region
